Edubridge

UI UX Designer, Frontend Developer

2023 - present

Overview

I had the pleasure to collaborate with EduBridge, a renowned educational institution devoted to supporting Indonesian students who choose to pursue their studies overseas.

In order to produce a visually appealing and user-friendly platform that was specifically targeted to the client's demands and objectives, I worked closely with them throughout the website's redesign and development phase of the project.

To website!

Challenges

Redesigning a fully operated website
every flow, every layout every chosen icon and image has been deployed to the live page, to redesign such website is a challenge for me because it's hard to implement a drastic change because time and effort was on the measure for the stakeholder

Complex Design Requirements
The design itself was quite challenging. There were specific layout requests that were new and unique for me to design, such as invoices, feedback forms, and other specialized elements.

Comprehensive Redesign
The needs extended beyond just a design revamp. It involved a complete overhaul of the branding and user experience (UX) flow, ensuring that the new design aligned with EduBridge's vision and goals.

Stakeholder Management
The stakeholders commenced a SCRUM meeting every week to review progress and provide feedback. There were multiple stakeholders, each with their own preferences for the design taste

Solutions

Redesigning a fully operated website
For every design that needs to be retouched, I always try to find the gaps so the change won't take much effort. I always start with color and images as a starting point; these two factors play a huge role in the quality of the design. Changing the layout can be tricky, but the urgency of improving the experience can help me enforce the necessary changes to my stakeholder.

Complex Design Requirements
A large amount of time spent on research and designing many alternatives has helped me a lot in achieving a decent design, which was initially unfamiliar to me. Asking a lot of questions also helps to ensure my design aligns closely with the goals of my stakeholders.

image of invoice, email and pdf cover design

Comprehensive Redesign
Tailoring the design to meet the specific needs of the target audience, such as making it attractive for teenagers and simple enough for parents, is crucial for the success of the project.

Stakeholder Management
Creating many alternative layouts and design to give sample to different stakeholder preferences can be challenging but is necessary for ensuring stakeholder satisfaction. Flexibility in design and being open to feedback are key to successful project delivery.

Lesson learned

  1. Designing for unique and complex requirements can be challenging, but it also provides an opportunity to learn and grow. Being adaptable and open to new ideas is essential.
  2. Conducting thorough UX research is crucial for creating designs that are user-centric and meet the needs of the target audience. It helps in making informed design decisions and ensures a better user experience.
  3. An iterative design process, with regular feedback loops, leads to a more refined and successful final product. Continuous improvements based on feedback are essential for meeting client expectations.
  4. Maintaining consistent branding across a website is important for creating a cohesive and professional look. Detailed branding guidelines help in achieving this consistency.
